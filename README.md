# Sales-Customer-and-Business-Performance-Dashboard

# Overview
This project demonstrates how a multi-page Power BI dashboard can be used to bring together sales, customer, product, and employee performance into a single, decision-ready view for business leaders.

Using Power BI, I analysed transactional and operational data to help businesses:

- Identify revenue drivers

- Understand customer value and buying behavior

- Optimize product and inventory decisions

- Evaluate sales team and office performance

The dashboard is designed for executives and managers, enabling faster, insight-driven decisions across departments.

# Business Problem

Growing businesses often face fragmented reporting across sales, customers, inventory, and teams.

Key challenges addressed:

- Which products and product lines drive the most revenue and profit?

- Which customers generate the highest value?

- Where is revenue being lost due to order issues or delays?

- How do sales reps and offices perform relative to each other?

- How can inventory and procurement be aligned with demand?

# Strategic Recommendations

- Reduce order friction for high-value product lines to prevent revenue leakage

- Build targeted CRM strategies for high-credit, high-value customers

- Align inventory planning with top-performing product lines

- Use sales-rep and office benchmarks to design incentive programs

- Monitor order status and shipping KPIs continuously via dashboards
